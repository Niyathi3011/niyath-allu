Why I'm Starting This Blog
February 8, 2026 · 2 min read

Hello, World

I've been working at the intersection of large language models, alignment, and evaluation for a while now — and I've learned that some of the most valuable insights come from the messy, in-between moments that never make it into a paper.

This blog is my space to share those insights: practical lessons from building agentic pipelines, thoughts on LLM evaluation, and reflections on where this field is heading.

What to Expect

I plan to write about topics like:

  • Prompt optimization — what actually works when your data is noisy or sparse
  • LLM-as-a-Judge — building evaluation systems that are both reliable and scalable
  • Alignment in practice — bridging the gap between research papers and production systems
  • Career reflections — navigating the AI industry as a researcher and engineer

Stay Tuned

If any of this resonates, stick around. I'll aim to keep posts concise and grounded in real experience rather than hype.
